5-1 Position

The 5-1 system is the most common volleyball formation. The "5" represents five attackers (Outside, Opposite, and Middle hitters), and the "1" is a single, dedicated setter who runs the offense in all six rotations. It offers highly consistent sets and better team chemistry, though the setter must be able to play both front and back rows.
